Individual Consultations

Going to therapy involves a deeply personal journey. It is not always easy, but it can be worth it for those who want to find the way to a more meaningful life and learn to deal with difficult thoughts and feelings, worry or anxiety. We will help you explore who you are and what is important to you. Together we will practice skills that will make your life better.

The psychologists at Nevermind are experts in psychological flexibility, avail scientific methods and have a large bank of experience. We know that starting therapy can be a big hurdle, and we will do our best to make it feel easy and comfortable for you. We will be there with you throughout the whole journey. Come as you Are.

Galanta Gruppen

We live in a society that is more connected than ever, where we have the opportunity to be in constant contact with each other. We have access to each other, regardless of time or place. And yet it is difficult to maintain contact with others! Many people recognize themselves in this paradox.

We psychologists know that the most important thing for a person is other people. We need meaningful relationships to feel good. Being good at creating and maintaining such relationships is a skill that can be trained just like riding a bike or cooking.

In this group we practice creating meaningful relationships and making them last.

Föräldrasamtal

We offer parenting sessions where we equip you with tools to have a good relationship with your child. We talk about challenges that may arise and give you skills to make parenting more enjoyable. The goal is to help you become a better parent and create a nice atmosphere for you and your child.

Studenthjälpen

Student life means a lot of freedom. Sometimes freedom can become a heavy burden. The sense of responsibility and uncertainty can be overwhelming. You can end up in a spiral of procrastination or lose motivation. It is not unusual to fall behind in studies. There can be way too much to do. Homesickness and the feeling of loneliness can be other challenges.

We at Nevermind have a great understanding and a lot of experience in all of this. We listen, help you sort and take steps towards a better life.
