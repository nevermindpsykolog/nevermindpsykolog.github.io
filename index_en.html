Individual consultations

Therapy is a deeply personal journey. It's not always easy, but it can be incredibly rewarding for those seeking a more meaningful life and learning to manage difficult thoughts and feelings, worry, or anxiety. We will help you explore who you are and what truly matters to you. Together, we will practice skills that can make your life better.

Couples therapy

A psychologist can help a couple find tools to manage challenges in their relationship. Together, they can build a foundation for a long-lasting relationship where both partners' needs are met.

There's no specific time to seek help. Relationships have their ups and downs; sometimes things feel better, sometimes worse. That's normal. If the difficult periods become longer and more frequent, it might be a good idea to seek help.

Couples seek help for various reasons. Some want to improve an already good relationship, others are unsure if they want to stay together, and some want to save their relationship. The important thing is to be motivated to reconnect with each other.

Online therapy

You can meet with a psychologist via your phone or computer. It's flexible and allows you to be anywhere. Online therapy is great if you have trouble visiting a clinic or want more convenient access. With video sessions, you can easily talk to your psychologist whenever and wherever it suits you best. Many people prefer discussing difficult topics from the comfort of their own home, where they feel safest.

If you live in a small town or have trouble finding a nearby psychologist, online therapy is a great option. It's also helpful if you prefer talking to a psychologist you don't know personally. Online therapy is ideal if you or your partner find it challenging to visit a clinic for any reason.

Parent counseling

We offer parent counseling sessions to equip you with tools for building a strong relationship with your child. We discuss potential challenges and provide skills to make parenting more enjoyable. Our goal is to help you become a better parent and create a good atmosphere for you and your child.

Student support

Student life offers a lot of freedom. Sometimes, that freedom can become a heavy burden. Feelings of responsibility and uncertainty can be overwhelming. It's easy to fall into a cycle of procrastination or lose motivation. Falling behind in your studies is not uncommon, and the workload can become too much to handle. Homesickness and loneliness are other common challenges.

At Nevermind, we understand these issues well and have extensive experience dealing with them. We listen, help you sort things out, and guide you toward a better life.

The relationship gym

Learn skills that benefit you both in the boardroom and the bedroom. Our focus is on maintaining connections, avoiding loneliness and isolation. We address social isolation, which isn't necessarily physical or geographic. We emphasize the importance of letting people know they matter to you. But how do you do that? Here, we learn together!

We practice courtesy training, active listening, validation, and supportive responses.

We live in a society that is more connected than ever, allowing us to be in constant contact with each other, anytime and anywhere. Yet, it's challenging to maintain those connections! Many people can relate to this paradox.

As psychologists, we know that the most important thing for a person is other people. We need meaningful relationships to thrive. Being skilled at creating and maintaining these relationships is a skill that can be developed, just like riding a bike or cooking.

In this group, we practice creating meaningful relationships and making them last.
